We Handle Everything. You Handle Nothing.

Permits, subs, materials, and finishes, fully managed.

"Full service" gets thrown around a lot in this industry. At Clarke Building, it means something specific: we pull the permits, schedule and manage every licensed subcontractor (electricians, plumbers, HVAC), source materials, guide finish selections, and coordinate inspections. You don't have to hire an electrician separately or figure out the permitting process on your own.

Clarke Building is licensed and insured in Massachusetts with a Home Improvement Contractor (HIC) license, General Liability insurance, and Workers' Compensation coverage. Every trade partner we bring onto your project is held to the same standard, because they work with us repeatedly and their reputation depends on it.
