Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of clarkebuildingcompany.com and all related web forms, email communications, phone calls, text messages (SMS), and other interactions with Clarke Building Company.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Project details: service area, type of work, project scope, photos, and project timeline
  • Communication records: emails, text messages, call logs, and form submissions

We also automatically collect limited technical information, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited (through Google Analytics 4).

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This service places cookies on your device to track page visits, session duration, and other usage patterns. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Provide estimates and project information
  • Manage and execute construction projects
  • Send project updates, reminders, and scheduling communications
  • Comply with legal and contractual obligations

Text Messages (SMS)

Program Name: Service Alerts

Message Types: Estimates, scheduling, job updates, reminders, and occasional promotions.

Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your project status.

Fees: Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier rates apply to all SMS messages. Not all plans include unlimited messaging.

Opt-Out and Opt-In Information: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just reply with the text "STOP" to any message from us at (978) 693-5706. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, we will not send you any further SMS messages unless you resubscribe by texting us back or re-opting in through a web form.

Help and Support: If you have questions about the Service Alerts program, reply with the text "HELP" or contact us directly at dave@clarkebuildingcompany.com or (978) 693-5706.

Consent Is Not a Condition of Purchase

Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing products or services from Clarke Building Company. You can request our services and obtain estimates without opting in to SMS communications.

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.
