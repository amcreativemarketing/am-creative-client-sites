Serving Topsfield, a Community Built on New England Craftsmanship

Topsfield is one of the North Shore's most well-preserved New England communities. Home to the Topsfield Fair, the oldest agricultural fair in the United States (established in 1818), it's a town where residents take real pride in their properties, their neighborhood, and the character that makes the area worth living in.

The homes here reflect that character. Topsfield is full of antique colonials, Cape Cods, historic Federals, and carefully maintained Victorians. These homes have great bones, but they also come with the realities of older construction: dated floor plans, deferred maintenance, and rooms that don't match how families live today. Working on them takes a contractor who understands older framing, plaster walls, original hardwood, and the importance of preserving what makes them special while improving how they function.

Clarke Building Company has worked on homes like these throughout the North Shore. We understand the construction methods, the common surprises behind the walls, and what it takes to get it right. Whether you're updating a single room or planning a full renovation, Dave will walk your home, talk through options, and build a scope that fits your goals and your budget.
