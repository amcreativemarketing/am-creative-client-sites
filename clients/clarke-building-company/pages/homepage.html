An Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) is a secondary living space on an existing residential property — such as an in-law suite, garage conversion, or backyard cottage. Massachusetts updated its ADU laws in 2024 to allow ADUs by-right on most single-family properties under 900 square feet.
