Dave Clarke

Owner & General Contractor

Building Runs in the Family

Dave Clarke grew up around job sites. His grandfather was a contractor. His father spent over 40 years building homes across Ipswich and the North Shore. After years working alongside his dad, Dave started Clarke Building Company to carry on that same standard of work - quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and treating every home like his own.

Today, Clarke Building Company is an owner-operated business based in West Newbury, MA. When you hire Dave, you get Dave. Not a rotating crew of project managers or salespeople you'll never see again. Dave is your single point of contact from the first phone call to the final walkthrough.

That's how his father did it. That's how he does it.

Why Clients Trust Dave With Their Homes

One Point of Contact

You'll talk to Dave. Not a receptionist, not a project coordinator - Dave. He's on your job site, managing every detail, and available when you have a question. Clients consistently praise the communication and organization that comes with working directly with the person in charge.

Honest and Straightforward

Dave will tell you exactly what a project involves - the timeline, the costs, and the process. If something is outside his expertise, he'll say so and bring in the right professional. No surprises, no runaround.

Deep Knowledge of How Homes Work

Three generations of building experience means Dave understands how every system in your home connects. That knowledge shows up in the details - catching problems before they become expensive, explaining your options clearly, and making sure the finished product lasts.

Family Business Values

Clarke Building Company is a family operation. Dave and his wife are raising three young kids while running the business. The people working on your home are professionals who care about doing things right - because their reputation is their livelihood.
