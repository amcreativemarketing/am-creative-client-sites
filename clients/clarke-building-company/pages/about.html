Clarke Building Company is a licensed, insured general contracting business based in West Newbury, Massachusetts. The company serves homeowners throughout the North Shore who want quality residential construction without the runaround that comes from larger firms.

Dave Clarke started this company on a straightforward premise: homeowners deserve a contractor who shows up, communicates clearly, and does the work right. No bait-and-switch on pricing. No strangers on your job site you've never been introduced to. No chasing someone down for an update.

If you're planning a custom home, an addition, a full renovation, or a structural project on an existing home, this is what working with Clarke Building Company looks like: you talk to Dave, you get an honest estimate, and Dave is on your job site from framing through finish work.

How Dave Clarke Got Into the Trade

Dave Clarke didn't stumble into construction - he grew up around it. From an early age he was drawn to the way a building comes together, the logic of framing, the problem-solving that happens before a single finish piece goes in. There's a satisfaction in that kind of work that's hard to explain to someone who hasn't done it: you can point at something solid and know it's there because you built it right.

He came up through the trades the old-fashioned way - working jobs, learning how different crews operate, figuring out what separates a project that runs smoothly from one that turns into a headache for everyone involved. Along the way he picked up a picture of what a general contractor should actually be: someone who manages the whole process with the homeowner's interests at the center, not just a scheduler who passes work off and hopes it comes together.

By the time Dave launched Clarke Building Company in West Newbury, he had a clear idea of the kind of contractor he wanted to be. Owner-operated. Personally accountable. The kind of builder where, if something's not right, you know exactly who to call - and that person is already on it.

Today, Clarke Building Company takes on custom homes, additions, renovations, and structural projects for homeowners across West Newbury and the surrounding North Shore communities. Every project is managed by Dave directly. He coordinates the subcontractors, he walks the job, he's the one who knows where the project stands on any given Wednesday morning.

What Working With Dave Actually Looks Like

Clear communication, honest pricing, and one person running your project from start to finish.

You'll Always Know Where Things Stand

You won't be left wondering what's happening on your project. Dave checks in proactively - a quick text, a call, a note after a sub finishes a phase. If something changes, you hear about it before it shows up as a surprise. Construction involves decisions, and Dave makes sure homeowners are part of the ones that matter.

Transparent Pricing, No Surprises

Dave puts together detailed, honest estimates. The goal isn't to win the job with a low number and work it back up - it's to give you a real picture of what the project costs so you can make an informed decision. If something comes up mid-project that affects the budget, you'll know why, and you'll know before it's committed.

One Person Running Your Job

When you hire Clarke Building Company, Dave Clarke is your contractor - not an office contact who passes messages to a crew you've never met. He manages the subs, he's on site, he carries the project in his head. The person you talked to on the phone is the same person driving to your house on Monday morning.

Steady Hands When Things Get Complicated

Construction projects hit snags. Lumber shows up wrong, a sub has a scheduling conflict, something unexpected opens up in a wall. Dave's been doing this long enough that surprises don't derail him. He works through problems methodically, keeps the project moving, and keeps homeowners out of the stress that can come with a renovation if the contractor isn't running a tight ship.
