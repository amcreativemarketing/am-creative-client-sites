## Positioning & Messaging

### Core Positioning

> **"When failure is not an option, we are the team to call."**

### Differentiator Stack (Priority Order)

1. **Reliability** - We show up and deliver (validated by competitor failures)
2. **Speed** - Fast turnaround in an industry of 3+ month waits
3. **Personal Service** - Tor answers the phone, not a call center
4. **Quality** - 3M and Avery materials, craftsmanship
5. **Full-Service** - One team from design to installation

## Voice & Tone

### Character

- Direct, no-BS, blue-collar professional
- Technical competence without jargon
- Practical, responsive, works around the clock
- Warm but not overly casual

### Do

- Emphasize speed and reliability (core differentiator)
- Be specific about what they do differently
- Mirror Tor's practical, responsive style
- Use customer language from reviews

### Don't

- Generic "quality craftsmanship" language
- Overpromise on turnaround times
- Sound like a franchise or call center
- Use marketing buzzwords
