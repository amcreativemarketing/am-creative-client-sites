Illuminated and LED Pylon Signs

An illuminated pylon sign works around the clock - drawing attention during the day and standing out against a dark sky at night. LED pylon signs are the most energy-efficient and longest-lasting option, cutting operating costs compared to older fluorescent and neon lighting.

  • Energy-efficient LED upgrades for existing pylon signs

  • Lower maintenance and operating costs vs. fluorescent

Pricing

How Much Do Pylon Signs Cost?

Pylon sign pricing varies widely depending on size, type, and features. A basic non-illuminated pole sign might start in the low thousands, while a large illuminated multi-tenant pylon sign can range well above $15,000. Here are the main factors that drive the cost.

Size and Height

Taller signs require deeper foundations, heavier steel, and more engineering. A 15-foot pole sign costs significantly less than a 40-foot highway pylon.

Illumination Type

Non-illuminated signs are the most affordable. LED-illuminated cabinet faces or channel letters add cost but deliver 24/7 visibility and stronger brand presence.

Single vs. Multi-Tenant

A multi-tenant directory sign with interchangeable panels is more complex to fabricate than a single-tenant pylon, adding to the overall project cost.

Permitting and Installation

Local sign codes, setback requirements, foundation excavation, and electrical work all factor into the installed cost. We handle the full permitting process for you.
