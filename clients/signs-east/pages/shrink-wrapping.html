Why Shrink Wrap Instead of a Tarp?

Tarps are cheap. They're also terrible. Here's why shrink wrap is worth it.

Custom Fit

Shrink wrap conforms to your boat's exact shape. No flapping, no pooling water, no gaps where snow and debris get in.

Weatherproof Seal

Heat-sealed seams create a watertight barrier. Rain, snow, and ice stay out - completely.

UV Protection

Our shrink wrap blocks harmful UV rays that fade gelcoat, crack vinyl, and degrade rubber seals.

Wind Resistant

Once shrunk, it stays put. No straps to tighten, no corners coming loose, no midnight emergencies.

Prevents Mold & Mildew

Ventilation openings allow airflow while keeping moisture out. Your boat stays dry inside, not damp.

Pest Protection

Sealed wrap keeps out mice, squirrels, birds, and insects that love to nest in stored boats.

How It Works

We come to you - your driveway, marina, or storage yard.

1

Schedule & Prep

Call or email us with your boat details (length, type, location). We'll schedule a time that works for you. Before we arrive, remove loose items and make sure the boat is accessible.

2

Frame Installation

We build a lightweight support frame to prevent water pooling and snow accumulation. The frame creates a peaked structure that sheds precipitation instead of collecting it.

3

Wrap & Heat Shrink

We cover your boat with industrial-grade shrink wrap, then heat it with propane torches to shrink the material tight. All seams are heat-welded for a complete seal.

4

Ventilation & Access

We install ventilation openings to prevent moisture buildup inside. If needed, we can add a zipper door for winter access. Your boat is now protected until spring.
