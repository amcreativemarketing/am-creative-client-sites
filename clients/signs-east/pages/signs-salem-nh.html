Do I Need a Sign Permit in Salem, NH?

Yes. Salem requires a permit from the Building Department before installing, replacing, or moving any commercial sign. The good news: sign repair and maintenance don't require a permit.

The application requires a sketch plan of your site, elevation drawings, and the sign caption. The permit fee is a flat $100. Salem uses the OpenGov online permitting system, so applications can be submitted digitally.

We've installed signs across Southern NH and know the process. One less thing you have to figure out on your own.
