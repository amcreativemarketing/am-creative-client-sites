The Craft of Neon

Real neon requires skill and patience. Here's what goes into every sign we build.

Hand-Bent Glass

Each tube is heated and shaped by hand over a ribbon burner. No two pieces are exactly alike - that's the character of real neon.

Gas-Filled Tubes

Neon gas glows red-orange. Argon with mercury vapor produces blue. Different phosphor coatings create additional colors.

Built In-House

We design and fabricate neon right here in Kingston - no outsourcing to overseas factories. Faster turnaround, better quality control.
