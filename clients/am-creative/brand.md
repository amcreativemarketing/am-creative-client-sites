# AM Creative - Brand Reference

## Colors
- Navy: #1a2b4a (headlines on light bg, section backgrounds)
- Gold: #b8976a (eyebrows, icons, links, accent elements)
- Gold Dark: #a07d4e (gradients, hover states)
- Light BG: #f8f9fa (alternating sections)
- Body Text: #4a5568 (paragraphs)

## Typography
- Eyebrow: 13px, 600 weight, uppercase, 2.5px letter-spacing
- Headlines: clamp(28px, 4vw, 42px), 700 weight
- Body: 16px, 1.8 line-height

## Voice & Tone
- Practical, no-nonsense
- Grade 7-9 readability
- Results-focused, not jargon-heavy
