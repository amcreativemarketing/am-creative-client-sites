The Challenge

Great at Building. Ready to Grow Beyond Referrals.

Bradford Construction had built an excellent reputation through word of mouth. Rick White and his team delivered premium renovation and ADU work across the North Shore. But referrals alone had a ceiling - and their online presence wasn't doing the heavy lifting it needed to.

Previous marketing attempts hadn't delivered. TV campaigns brought minimal results. Print magazine ads weren't moving the needle. Multiple agencies came and went, delivering reports but never the phone calls that actually matter.

  • Traditional advertising that wasn't generating real leads
  • Past agencies that delivered reports, not booked jobs
  • ADU program existed but wasn't converting online
  • Strong reputation offline, but limited visibility in search results
  • No cohesive digital presence or lead generation system

"I'd tried TV, print magazines - spent real money and didn't see results. They call me now and I'm like, don't ever talk to me again."

Rick White, Owner
The Strategy

A Marketing System Built for a Growing Contractor

We didn't throw money at ads and hope. We built an integrated marketing system designed to generate qualified leads and position Bradford as the premium choice.

1
Months 1-2: Foundation

Know the Customer, Nail the Message

We started with deep Voice of Customer research, not ad spend. We identified Bradford's unique positioning - "The only builder who never leaves" - combining construction excellence with lifetime home services. From there we built a new conversion-focused website to give every growth strategy the best possible starting position, set up Google Business Profile, and implemented AM Connect for automated lead management.

2
Months 3-6: ADU Market Domination

Own a Niche Before Going Wide

We built a comprehensive ADU landing page and digital lookbook, then drove traffic through targeted Meta ads. The messaging centered on "Dignity of Proximity" for aging-in-place families. AM Connect automated follow-up so no lead sat unanswered. The ADU page quickly became the 2nd most visited page on the entire site.

3
Months 6-12: Content & Authority

Make Them the Expert Everyone Talks About

We built a content engine around active job sites, educational posts, and client success stories. Social media went from sporadic to strategic - 8-10 posts per month positioning Rick as the North Shore ADU authority. Combined with local SEO optimization, Bradford started showing up everywhere their ideal clients looked.

4
Month 12+: Scale & Expand

Pour Fuel on What's Working

With the system proven, Rick made the call to invest more aggressively into marketing for 2026 - a major shift in mindset. We expanded to Google Ads, began geographic expansion into NH and Western Mass, and refined lead qualification to focus exclusively on premium projects. Industry pros now approach Rick unprompted, telling him they see Bradford as the go-to for premium ADU work.
