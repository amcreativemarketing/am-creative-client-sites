Why West Newbury Homeowners Choose Chain Link Fencing

Chain link fencing remains one of the most practical and cost-effective options for West Newbury properties. It delivers the security and boundary definition homeowners need without the higher price tag of wood or vinyl fencing. For Essex County properties that deal with freeze-thaw cycles and coastal air, galvanized chain link holds up remarkably well with minimal upkeep.

Whether you need pet containment along the Artichoke River corridor, child safety around a pool, or clear property boundaries that don't block sightlines or natural light, chain link is a reliable choice. Chain Link Fence Options Available in West Newbury

We carry a full range of chain link fencing products to match your property's needs, budget, and aesthetic preferences. Every installation is customized to the terrain and layout of your West Newbury lot.

Residential Heights and Styles

Standard residential chain link is available in 3 ft, 4 ft, and 6 ft heights. Choose from galvanized steel for the most economical option, or upgrade to vinyl-coated chain link in black, green, or brown to blend with your landscaping. Privacy slats can be added for extra screening along property lines. Gate options include single walk gates for side yards and double drive gates for driveways and equipment access.

Commercial and Security-Grade Options

For commercial properties, we install 8 ft to 10 ft+ security-grade chain link with heavier wire gauge for maximum durability. Add barbed wire or razor ribbon for construction sites, storage facilities, and municipal properties that require enhanced security. All commercial installations meet Massachusetts building code requirements.
