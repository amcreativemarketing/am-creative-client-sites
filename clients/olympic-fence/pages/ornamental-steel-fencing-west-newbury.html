Frequently Asked Questions About Ornamental Fencing in West Newbury

Common questions from West Newbury property owners considering ornamental aluminum or steel fencing.

Do I need a permit for a fence in West Newbury, MA?

Fences under 7 feet in height typically do not require a building permit in West Newbury. Most residential ornamental fencing falls well within this limit. For pool fencing, Massachusetts state code requires a minimum height of 48 inches with self-closing, self-latching gates regardless of permit requirements. Olympic Fence handles all compliance details for your project.

What is the difference between aluminum and steel ornamental fencing?

Aluminum fencing is naturally rust-proof, lighter weight, and requires zero maintenance - making it ideal for residential properties, pool enclosures, and Merrimack riverfront lots. Steel fencing provides maximum strength with custom ornamental detailing, galvanized plus powder-coated for 30+ year protection. Steel is the better choice for estate perimeters, equestrian properties, and security applications. Both carry our lifetime workmanship warranty.

Can ornamental fencing be installed on West Newbury's hilly terrain?

Yes. West Newbury is well known for its rolling hills, and ornamental fencing can be racked (angled) to follow slopes or stair-stepped for steeper grades. With over 40 years of experience installing fences across West Newbury's terrain, our crews plan proper post placement and panel adjustment to ensure a clean, professional look on any grade.

How long does ornamental fence installation take?

Most residential ornamental fence installations in West Newbury are completed in 2-4 days, depending on lot size and terrain. Larger estate properties with extensive fence lines may take slightly longer. We provide a clear timeline during your free consultation so there are no surprises.

What does the lifetime workmanship warranty cover?

Our lifetime workmanship warranty covers our installation work for as long as you own your property. If any issue arises from how the fence was installed, we come back and make it right at no cost. Most fence companies only offer 2-3 year warranties. As a West Newbury-based company, we stand behind our work because our reputation depends on it.

Does ornamental fencing work for pool enclosures in Massachusetts?

Absolutely. Aluminum ornamental fencing is one of the most popular choices for pool enclosures in Massachusetts. We install pool-grade panels that meet state code requirements, including minimum 48-inch height, maximum 4-inch picket spacing, and self-closing, self-latching gates. The result looks far more elegant than standard chain link while providing full compliance.
