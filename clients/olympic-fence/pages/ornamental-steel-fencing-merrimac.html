Do I need a fence permit in Merrimac, MA?

Fences under 7 feet in height typically do not require a building permit in Merrimac. Most residential ornamental fencing is 4-6 feet tall and falls well within this limit. Pool fencing must meet Massachusetts state code regardless of permit requirements, including minimum 48-inch height and self-closing, self-latching gates. Olympic Fence handles all compliance details for you.

What is the difference between aluminum and steel ornamental fencing?

Aluminum fencing is naturally rust-proof and maintenance-free, making it ideal for residential yards, pool enclosures, and properties near Lake Attitash or the Merrimack River. Steel fencing delivers maximum strength and allows custom ornamental detailing with 30+ year corrosion protection. Both materials carry our lifetime workmanship warranty.

Is ornamental fencing safe for families with children and pets?

Yes. Ornamental aluminum and steel fencing provides excellent containment for children and pets. Tight picket spacing prevents smaller animals from slipping through, and the height options we install keep larger dogs safely contained. For Merrimac families, it offers peace of mind while maintaining the clean, open look that suits the town's residential character. We also install dedicated dog fencing solutions.

Will ornamental fencing hold up near Lake Attitash and the Merrimack River?

Absolutely. Aluminum fencing is naturally rust-proof and handles moisture exposure without any coating or special treatment, making it the top choice for Lake Attitash waterfront and Merrimack River-adjacent properties.
