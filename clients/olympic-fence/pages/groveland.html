Frequently Asked Questions - Groveland Fencing

Do I need a permit for a fence in Groveland?
In Groveland, fences 6 feet or under typically don't require a building permit. However, properties near the Merrimack River or wetland areas may need Conservation Commission approval. We verify all requirements for your specific property and handle permit applications when needed.
How long does fence installation take in Groveland?
Most residential fences in Groveland are completed within 2-4 days, depending on the size and terrain. Properties near Veasey Park or the Merrimack River may require additional preparation. During peak season (late summer through fall), scheduling varies, but we offer temporary fencing solutions if you need immediate containment.
What's the best fence material for Groveland properties?
For Groveland's varied terrain, we recommend our wood-on-steel cedar systems for durability and aesthetics, or vinyl fencing for low maintenance near the Merrimack River. Cedar lasts 15-30 years with proper care, while vinyl can exceed 35 years. Our steel-post installation handles New England's freeze-thaw cycles better than traditional wood posts.
Can you install fences on sloped properties in Groveland?
Absolutely. Groveland has many properties with challenging grades, especially near Crane Pond and the East Parish area. We offer both stepped installations (panels follow the slope in steps) and racked/contoured installations (panels follow the ground slope) based on your preference and the terrain.
What sets Olympic Fence apart from other Groveland fence companies?
Three things: our lifetime workmanship warranty (most competitors offer 2-3 years), our proprietary wood-on-steel installation system that prevents post rot, and our 40 years of local experience. We're family-owned, with two generations of fence expertise serving the Merrimack Valley.
Do you offer fence repairs in Groveland?
Yes, we repair all fence types regardless of who installed them. Storm damage, rotted posts, broken gates, and general wear are all services we provide. Many Groveland homeowners call us after nor'easters or heavy snow damage.
How much does a fence cost in Groveland, MA?
Fence pricing depends on material, linear footage, terrain, and any gates or custom features. Rather than give a misleading range, we provide free on-site estimates that account for your specific property. We work within various budgets and can suggest options to maximize value without sacrificing quality.
Do you serve commercial properties in Groveland?
Yes, we handle commercial fencing projects including security fencing, athletic fields, and municipal installations. Our commercial service area extends 70 miles from West Newbury, easily covering all of Groveland and the surrounding Merrimack Valley.
