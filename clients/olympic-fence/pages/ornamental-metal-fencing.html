Quick Decision Guide

Choose Aluminum if: You need pool safety fencing, want the lowest maintenance option, live in a coastal area with salt air, prefer a budget-conscious solution, or prioritize lightweight installation.

Choose Steel if: Maximum security is required, you have a commercial or municipal property, the fence faces high-impact risks, you want custom ornamental designs, or need the absolute strongest fencing available.
