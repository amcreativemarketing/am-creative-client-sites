Fencing Services Throughout Beverly

We know Beverly's neighborhoods, coastal terrain, and local requirements. Here's how we serve each area:

Downtown & Cabot Street District

Historic properties along Cabot Street and downtown Beverly often require fencing that complements traditional New England architecture. We specialize in classic cedar designs and painted picket fences that meet the aesthetic expectations of Beverly's historic downtown while providing modern durability. Many properties in this walkable district need privacy solutions that balance streetscape aesthetics with backyard security.

Beverly Cove & Waterfront

Properties near Lynch Park, Beverly Harbor, and Independence Park face unique challenges from coastal exposure, salt air, and moisture. Our vinyl fencing excels in these conditions with 35+ year lifespans and zero maintenance requirements. We handle waterfront properties throughout Beverly Cove, Hospital Point, and the Bass River area, with experience navigating Conservation Commission requirements for properties near wetland buffers.

Beverly Farms & Route 127

The eastern areas of Beverly feature larger lots, wooded properties, and the quintessentially New England character that inspired Beverly Hills, California. We handle privacy fencing for secluded estates, pool enclosures for family properties, and agricultural fencing for properties with acreage. The Beverly Commons Conservation Area (formerly Witches' Woods) runs through many neighborhoods here, requiring careful attention to environmental regulations.

North Beverly & Endicott College Area

Properties along Route 62 and the northern sections of Beverly see a mix of suburban homes and larger lots near Wenham Lake. We install everything from neighborhood-friendly privacy fencing to property boundary solutions for larger parcels. Pet containment and backyard pool enclosures are popular requests in this family-friendly area.
