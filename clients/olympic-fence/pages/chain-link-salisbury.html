Why Chain Link Is a Smart Choice for Salisbury's Coastal Properties

Living near Salisbury Beach State Reservation and the Merrimack River means elevated salt air exposure year-round. Salt accelerates oxidation and rust on untreated metal fencing, which is why material selection matters more here than it does for inland properties. Olympic Fence Co. installs two types of chain link fencing specifically suited to Salisbury's coastal conditions.

Galvanized Chain Link for Coastal Durability

Hot-dip galvanized chain link uses a zinc barrier coating to resist rust and corrosion from salt air. It's the standard for most residential and commercial installations in coastal Essex County. Galvanized chain link delivers proven performance at a lower price point and holds up well in Salisbury's freeze-thaw cycles, nor'easters, and coastal storms.

Vinyl-Coated Chain Link - Protection Plus Curb Appeal

Vinyl-coated chain link adds a polyethylene outer layer over galvanized steel, available in black, green, and brown. This double layer of protection is especially valuable for properties near Salisbury Beach or along the Merrimack River waterfront. The coating also gives a cleaner, more finished appearance - a popular choice for residential properties and vacation rentals that see seasonal tenants.

Not sure which material is right for your property? Our team evaluates every site individually and recommends the best option based on your property's distance from the water, exposure, and budget. If you're considering other fencing materials, we also install vinyl fencing, cedar fencing, and privacy fencing across Salisbury.
