Do I Need a Permit for My Haverhill Fence?

Yes. The City of Haverhill requires building permits for all fence installations, regardless of height. We guide you through the three-step process:

  1. Obtain a plot plan from the City Engineer's Office (Room 300, City Hall)
  2. Get Conservation Department approval and stamp (Room 300, City Hall)
  3. Apply for building permit at the Building Department (Room 210, City Hall)

Typical permit processing adds 2-3 weeks to your project start date. We help coordinate this process to keep your project on schedule.
