Common Questions

What are your cedar fence installation options?

We offer several options: Traditional Installation with wood posts set in concrete, Wood-on-Steel where cedar panels are attached to durable steel posts to prevent rot, and No-Dig Installation which uses driven steel anchors for a less invasive process.

How long will a cedar fence last?

Properly installed cedar, especially with our rot-preventing techniques like steel posts, can easily last 20-30 years or more in New England.

Should I stain or seal my fence?

While not required, staining helps maintain the cedar's color and sealing reduces water absorption. We can help you decide what's best for your property and budget.
