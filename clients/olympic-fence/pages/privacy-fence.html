Privacy Fence Height & Permit Guide for North Shore MA

Quick Answer: Most privacy fences are 6 feet tall and typically don't require permits in our service area.

Local Regulations:

  • Standard Height: 6 feet for backyard privacy fences (no permit required in most towns)
  • Front Yard: Usually limited to 3-4 feet (check with your town)
  • Corner Lots: May have sight-line restrictions
  • Pool Fences: Must be 4+ feet with specific gate requirements

We Handle the Details: Not sure about your town's rules? We pull all necessary permits and ensure your fence meets local codes. We've worked with planning boards in Newburyport, Haverhill, Amesbury, and throughout Essex County.

Common Privacy Fence Questions

How much does a privacy fence cost in Massachusetts?

Privacy fence costs vary based on material, height, and property specifics. Cedar typically offers the best entry price point, vinyl provides the best long-term value with zero maintenance, and our exclusive Wood-on-Steel system delivers premium durability. Most 6-foot privacy fences for average yards range from budget-friendly to premium options. We provide transparent quotes with payment plans available.

Do I need a permit for a 6-foot privacy fence?

In most North Shore towns, privacy fences 6 feet or under don't require permits for backyard installation. However, front yards, corner lots, and fences over 6 feet often need approval. We handle all permit applications for you and know the specific requirements for each town we serve.

How long does privacy fence installation take?

Most residential privacy fence installations take 2-4 days, depending on length and site conditions. A typical 150-200 foot privacy fence is usually completed in 3 days. We provide a clear timeline upfront and stick to it. Current scheduling is 3-4 weeks out during our busy season.

Which privacy fence material lasts longest?

Vinyl privacy fences last 35+ years with zero maintenance. Our Wood-on-Steel system lasts 30+ years by preventing ground-level rot. Traditional cedar lasts 15-30 years with periodic staining. We'll help you choose based on your priorities: looks, maintenance, and budget.

Can you install privacy fences on slopes or uneven ground?

Yes, we specialize in challenging installations. Whether your property has slopes, ledges, or uneven terrain, we have solutions. We can step the fence, follow the contour, or combine techniques for the best appearance and function. Many competitors won't tackle difficult properties - we see them as opportunities to showcase our expertise.
