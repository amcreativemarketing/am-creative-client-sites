Choosing Between Aluminum and Steel Fencing in Salisbury

Both aluminum and steel deliver outstanding durability and curb appeal, but each material has strengths that match different Salisbury property types. Here's how they compare for coastal applications.

Feature Aluminum Steel
Salt-Air Resistance Naturally rust-proof - no coating needed Protected by galvanized + powder coating
Best For Pools, residential, beachfront properties Commercial, high-security, Route 1 businesses
Strength Lightweight, flexible in coastal winds Maximum impact resistance
Maintenance Zero - occasional rinse with garden hose Minimal - annual visual inspection
Lifespan 25+ years 30+ years
Warranty Lifetime workmanship warranty from Olympic Fence

Quick guide: Choose aluminum if you're near Salisbury Beach, need pool-safe fencing, or want the lowest-maintenance option. Choose steel if you need maximum security for a commercial property, want custom ornamental designs, or need a fence that can handle high-impact situations.
