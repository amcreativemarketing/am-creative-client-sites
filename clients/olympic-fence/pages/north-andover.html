North Andover Fence Installation FAQs

Do I need a permit for a fence in North Andover?
Fences 6 feet or under typically don't require a permit in North Andover. For taller fences, contact the Building Department at (978) 688-9501. We guide you through the process and ensure compliance.
How long does fence installation take?
Most residential installations take 2-4 days once work begins. During peak season (late summer/fall), scheduling typically takes 3-4 weeks. We offer temporary fencing if you need security sooner.
What materials work best for North Andover's climate?
For harsh winters and humid summers, we recommend coastal-grade vinyl or our wood-on-steel cedar system. Vinyl excels near water (Lake Cochichewick, Merrimack River). Wood-on-steel is perfect for rocky soil near Harold Parker State Forest.
Can you install fences on sloped or rocky properties?
Yes! We specialize in stepped and contoured installations for hilly terrain. Our wood-on-steel system penetrates rocky soil more effectively than traditional posts. We'll assess your property during the free estimate.
What are your business hours?
Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM. Call (978) 997-2302 or submit an estimate request anytime - we respond within 48 hours. We also schedule estimates on evenings and weekends.
