Do You Need a Fence Permit in Merrimac, MA?

Merrimac Fence Permit Overview

In Merrimac, MA, most residential chain link fences under 6 feet in height do not require a building permit, but zoning setback rules from property lines and roads do apply. For fences in front yards or near lot lines, checking with the Merrimac Building Department is recommended before installation. Corner lots and properties near public roads may have additional restrictions.
