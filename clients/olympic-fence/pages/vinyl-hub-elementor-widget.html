Common Questions

How long does vinyl fencing last?

With minimal maintenance, a high-quality vinyl fence can last 35 years or more, making it an excellent long-term investment.

Will vinyl fade or turn yellow?

Modern professional-grade vinyl is manufactured with UV inhibitors, like titanium dioxide, to protect it from fading and yellowing caused by the sun. It will retain its color for many years.
