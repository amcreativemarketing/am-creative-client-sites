Signs Your Dryer Vent Needs Cleaning

If you notice any of these signs, your dryer vent likely has a lint buildup that needs professional attention:

01

Clothes Take Longer to Dry

Loads require two or more cycles to fully dry, even on high heat settings. This is the most common early warning sign.

02

Burning Smell During a Cycle

A burning or musty odor while your dryer runs, often caused by lint trapped near the heating element or compacted in the vent line.

03

Excessive Lint Buildup

Lint collecting around the dryer vent opening, lint trap area, door seal, or the exterior vent cap outside your home.

04

Dryer Feels Hot to the Touch

The top or sides of your dryer are unusually hot after a normal cycle, indicating restricted airflow and heat buildup.

05

Excess Humidity in Laundry Room

Your laundry room feels warm and humid while the dryer runs, a sign that moist air isn't exhausting properly.

06

Blocked Exterior Vent Hood

The vent flap outside your home doesn't open when the dryer is running, meaning airflow is severely restricted.

Benefits of Professional Dryer Vent Cleaning
