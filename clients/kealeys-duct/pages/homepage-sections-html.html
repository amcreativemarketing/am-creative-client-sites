Residential Air Duct & HVAC Cleaning

Your HVAC system circulates air through every room in the house. Over time, dust, pollen, pet dander, and construction debris collect in those ducts and get pushed back into your living space every time the system runs. We clean the full duct system, register to register, so your family breathes cleaner air.

  • Reduces dust, allergens, and pet dander circulation
  • Restores HVAC airflow and system efficiency
  • Recommended after renovations or new home purchases
  • Thorough photo documentation of the full cleaning

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we hear from homeowners.

Most homeowners should have their dryer vent professionally cleaned once a year. If your household runs more than five loads a week, or if you notice your dryer taking longer than it used to, every six months is a safer interval. Annual cleaning is the single most effective way to reduce dryer-related fire risk.

The most common signs are clothes taking more than one cycle to dry, a burning smell during use, the dryer drum feeling unusually hot at the end of a cycle, and visible lint around the outdoor vent opening. If you notice any of these, schedule a cleaning sooner rather than later.
