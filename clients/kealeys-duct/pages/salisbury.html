The EPA recommends cleaning air ducts "as needed" when there are specific issues like visible mold, pest infestations, or excessive dust. For most Salisbury homes, we recommend cleaning every 3-5 years. However, homes with pets, smokers, recent renovations, or family members with allergies may benefit from more frequent cleaning (every 2-3 years). Coastal humidity in Salisbury can also contribute to faster dust accumulation.

We recommend professional dryer vent cleaning every 12-18 months for most households. If you do multiple loads per week, have a long vent run, or notice your dryer taking longer to dry clothes, you may need more frequent cleaning (every 6-12 months). According to the U.S. Fire Administration, failure to clean dryer vents contributes to an estimated 2,900 home fires annually.
