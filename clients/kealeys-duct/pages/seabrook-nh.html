Warning Signs Your Dryer Vent Needs Cleaning

Don't wait for a problem to become an emergency. Watch for these common warning signs in your Seabrook home:

⚠
Clothes take more than one cycle to dry - this is often the first sign of restricted airflow.
⚠
Dryer feels extremely hot to the touch - excessive heat buildup indicates poor ventilation and fire risk.
⚠
Burning smell during operation - this is a serious warning sign that should never be ignored.
⚠
Laundry room feels hot and humid - trapped moisture and poor ventilation need immediate attention.
⚠
Visible lint at the exterior vent opening - if you can see buildup outside, the inside is likely much worse.
⚠
It's been over a year since professional cleaning - annual service is the minimum recommendation.

If you're experiencing any of these warning signs, contact us today to schedule your dryer vent cleaning before a small issue becomes a dangerous one.
