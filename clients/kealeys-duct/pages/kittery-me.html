Are There Signs That Indicate Your Air Ducts Need Cleaning?

Visible dust blowing from vents when the system turns on
If you notice dust puffs or particles each time your HVAC cycles on, there's significant buildup inside the ductwork that's being pushed into your living spaces with every cycle.
Musty or stale odors when the heating or cooling system runs
Persistent odors typically indicate mold growth or organic matter decaying inside the duct system â€” particularly common in Kittery's humid coastal climate. A professional inspection can identify and resolve the source.
Increased allergy symptoms or respiratory irritation indoors
If household members notice worsening allergy or asthma symptoms primarily when indoors, contaminated ductwork is a likely contributor. Cleaning removes the source of airborne irritants circulating through your home.
Uneven airflow or rooms that won't reach the set temperature
Restricted airflow from buildup inside the ducts can cause certain rooms to receive less conditioned air than others. This forces your HVAC system to work harder and can lead to premature equipment failure.
Visible mold around vent registers or inside accessible ductwork
Any visible mold warrants an immediate professional inspection and cleaning. Mold spores circulate through the entire duct system and can affect every room in your home. Learn about the health benefits of addressing mold in your ductwork.
Higher-than-expected energy bills with no change in usage
When your HVAC system has to work harder to push air through contaminated ducts, energy consumption rises. If your bills are climbing without an obvious explanation, dirty ductwork may be the cause. See how clean air ducts reduce energy bills.
