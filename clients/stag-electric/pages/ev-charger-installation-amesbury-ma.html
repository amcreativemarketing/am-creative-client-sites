How Do Electrical Panel Upgrades Support EV Charger Installation?

Some homes need electrical panel upgrades to handle the additional load of a new charger. During the site visit, Stag Electric evaluates the existing service panel and recommends the safest, code-compliant solution.

Common scenarios requiring upgrades include older homes with 100-amp service, panels with no available breaker slots, or homes with existing high-demand appliances. Taking care of panel issues up front ensures your charger runs reliably and safely.
