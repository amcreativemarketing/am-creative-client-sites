## Fonts
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (serif) - 400, 500, 600 weights + italic
- **Body:** Source Sans 3 (sans-serif) - 300, 400, 500, 600 weights
- **Squarespace site uses similar serif/sans pairing**

## Voice & Tone
- Warm, professional, compassionate
- Patient-first language
- Emphasizes: access, personalized care, no bureaucracy, relationship-based medicine
- Avoids: clinical jargon, insurance-speak, corporate healthcare tone
- Key phrases: "healthcare simplified," "enhanced access," "meaningful care," "no hidden costs"
