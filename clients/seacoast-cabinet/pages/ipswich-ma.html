Should I reface or replace my kitchen cabinets?

Refacing works best when your cabinet boxes are structurally sound and the current layout functions well for your family. It saves 40-60% compared to full replacement and typically takes just 3-5 days. Replacement makes more sense if boxes are damaged, you want a completely new layout, or the existing configuration no longer meets your needs.
