Kitchen Cabinet Questions from Amesbury Homeowners

How far is the Seacoast Cabinet showroom from Amesbury?

Our Rowley showroom is roughly 15 minutes south of Amesbury via I-95. We're located at 300 Newburyport Turnpike, Unit 3, Rowley, MA 01969. Walk-ins are welcome, though we recommend scheduling an appointment so a designer is available to give you focused attention.

What's the typical timeline for an Amesbury kitchen cabinet project?

Most projects take 4-8 weeks from order to delivery, depending on the cabinet line and level of customization. Homecrest orders tend to arrive on the faster end, while fully custom Omega orders may take closer to 8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline estimate during the proposal stage.

Do you handle kitchen remodeling in Essex County, or just cabinets?

We specialize in kitchen design, custom cabinetry, and countertop selection. We don't do general contracting, but we work closely with contractors and builders throughout Essex County and the Merrimack Valley. If you need a referral to a trusted contractor for your kitchen remodel, we're happy to connect you.

Can you work with my existing contractor on an Amesbury project?

Absolutely. We regularly coordinate with general contractors, builders, and interior designers on kitchen projects. We provide detailed cabinet specifications, installation guides, and direct designer communication so your contractor has everything they need for a clean install.

Does Seacoast Cabinet offer cabinet refacing in Amesbury?

We focus on new custom cabinetry rather than refacing. If your existing cabinet boxes are in good shape but you want updated doors and hardware, we can discuss whether a partial replacement makes sense for your project. In most cases, new cabinetry offers better long-term value, especially with our Homecrest line at competitive price points.

Are all your cabinet lines really made in the United States?

Yes. Homecrest, Mantra, and Omega are all manufactured domestically by MasterBrand Cabinets. This means more predictable lead times, no tariff-related price fluctuations, and direct quality control that imported cabinets can't match. Every cabinet we order for Amesbury homeowners is built in an American facility.
