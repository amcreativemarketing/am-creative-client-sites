Giving Back: Community Alliance for Teen Safety

This wonderful nonprofit is near and dear to our heart. Frank serves on the Board of Directors and leads as their President. CATS promotes safe habits for all youth by increasing awareness of high-risk behaviors, encouraging healthy choices, and fostering community alliances.

In support of the organization's mission, Frank runs a service-based program that introduces teens to the woodworking trade. Through this program, Frank and his mentees craft "Growth Rings" - small, connected wooden rings decorated or stained and tied together with paracord. Completed Growth Rings are then distributed as an act of random kindness throughout the community.

It's this same spirit of mentorship and community that drives everything we do at Seacoast Cabinet - whether we're helping a family design their dream kitchen or training the next generation of skilled tradespeople.
