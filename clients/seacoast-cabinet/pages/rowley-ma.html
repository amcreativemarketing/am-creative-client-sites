Popular Cabinet Styles for Rowley Homes

North Shore homes range from historic New England colonials to contemporary builds. Here are the cabinet styles our Rowley clients choose most often.

Shaker Style

The most requested style on the North Shore. Clean lines, recessed center panel, and a versatile look that works in traditional and modern kitchens alike. Shaker cabinets pair well with almost any hardware and countertop combination.

Raised Panel Traditional

A classic choice for Rowley's colonial and Federal-style homes. Raised panel doors add dimension and formality, especially in rich wood stains or glazed finishes that complement historic architecture.

Flat Panel Contemporary

Sleek, minimal, and modern. Flat panel (slab) doors create a streamlined look that's perfect for contemporary builds or homeowners who want a clean, uncluttered kitchen aesthetic.

Transitional Design

Blends traditional warmth with modern simplicity. Transitional cabinets are the sweet spot for homeowners who don't want anything too ornate or too minimal. This style dominates new builds across Essex County.

Glass Front Options

Glass-front cabinet doors add visual depth and allow you to display dishware, glassware, or decorative items. Available in clear, frosted, or textured glass across all our cabinet lines.

Open Shelving Accents

Open shelving mixed with closed cabinetry creates a relaxed, functional look that's popular in North Shore coastal kitchens. Great for displaying cookbooks, plants, or frequently used items.

Kitchen Cabinet FAQs

How much do custom kitchen cabinets cost in Rowley, MA?

Kitchen cabinet costs vary based on the size of your kitchen, the materials you choose, and how much customization you need. Semi-custom cabinets from Homecrest or Mantra offer excellent quality at moderate price points, while Omega's fully custom line provides maximum design freedom. We offer a free design consultation where we can discuss your budget and help you find the best options for your home. You can also explore our budget planning tool for a general starting point.

What's the difference between custom and semi-custom cabinets?

Semi-custom cabinets start with standard sizes and offer a generous range of finishes, door styles, and modification options. Fully custom cabinets are built to your exact dimensions and specifications, with no design limitations. Both deliver American-made quality. Our designers can help you decide which approach best fits your Rowley kitchen and budget.

How long does cabinet installation take?

A straightforward cabinet replacement typically takes a few days of on-site work. Larger renovations involving layout changes, countertops, and custom modifications may take several weeks from design to final walkthrough. Kitchen remodeling in Rowley typically follows a clear timeline we'll map out during your consultation so you know exactly what to expect.
