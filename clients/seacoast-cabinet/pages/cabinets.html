Shaker cabinets remain the most requested door style in our showroom, and across the North Shore. Custom vs. Semi-Custom: What's Right for Your Remodel?

Semi-custom cabinets give you hundreds of door styles, finishes, and modifications at a predictable price point. Fully custom options open up unlimited sizing and specialty features. Our design consultations help you determine which level of customization your project actually needs.
