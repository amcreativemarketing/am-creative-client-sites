Common Questions About Our Design Services

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll tour our showroom (or visit your home), take measurements of your space, discuss your vision and budget, and help you explore cabinet styles and countertop options. There's no obligation - it's simply a chance to see if we're a good fit.

Can I work with you if I have my own contractor?

Absolutely. Many of our clients bring their own contractors or installers. We provide detailed specifications and can coordinate directly with your contractor to ensure everything goes smoothly.

How do the design fee credits work?

Every dollar you pay for design services ($295 design package, hourly revisions, or retainer deposits) is credited directly toward your cabinet purchase. When you move forward with Seacoast Cabinet, the design is essentially free.

What if I can't visit the showroom?

No problem! Our virtual design service brings the showroom experience to you. We'll work remotely using photos and measurements to create your custom design and 3D renderings.

How long does the design process take?

Most designs are completed within 1-2 weeks after your initial design appointment. Timeline depends on project complexity and how quickly decisions are made. View our full process overview for details.

Do you only design kitchens?

While kitchens are our specialty, we also design bathrooms, laundry rooms, mudrooms, and any space that needs cabinetry. Our same design process and fee structure applies to all projects.
