How Our Design Fees Work

We've structured our fees to make professional design accessible—and to reward you when you move forward with your project.

Initial Consultation

Free

Your first meeting with us is always complimentary. We'll spend up to an hour together—either at our Rowley showroom or in your home—taking measurements, discussing your vision, and exploring cabinet styles and countertop options. There's no obligation, and you'll leave with a clear understanding of what's possible for your space.

Professional Design & 3D Renderings

$295

Once you're ready to move forward, this fee covers your custom design package: professional 3D renderings of your space, a detailed project estimate, and a dedicated 2-hour showroom appointment with your designer. You'll see exactly what your new kitchen will look like before making any commitments.

This fee is fully credited toward your cabinet purchase when you choose Seacoast Cabinet.

Design Revisions

$95/hour

Need to make changes after seeing your initial design? No problem. Additional revision time is billed hourly, and we recommend combining revision requests for efficiency. Whether you want to explore different layouts, finishes, or configurations, we'll refine until you're completely satisfied.

All revision fees are also credited toward your purchase.

Detailed Construction Plans

20% Retainer

For projects requiring detailed construction plans and specifications, we collect a 20% retainer based on your estimated cabinetry cost. This covers the additional documentation your contractor needs for installation.

Like all our design fees, this retainer is fully credited to your purchase when you move forward with Seacoast Cabinet.

The Bottom Line

Every dollar you invest in design services comes back to you when you purchase your cabinets through Seacoast Cabinet. We've structured it this way because we want you to have access to professional design help without feeling like you're paying twice.

Common Questions About Our Design Services

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll tour our showroom (or visit your home), take measurements of your space, discuss your vision and budget, and help you explore cabinet styles and countertop options. There's no obligation - it's simply a chance to see if we're a good fit.

Can I work with you if I have my own contractor?

Absolutely. Many of our clients bring their own contractors or installers. We provide detailed specifications and can coordinate directly with your contractor to ensure everything goes smoothly.

How do the design fee credits work?

Every dollar you pay for design services ($295 design package, hourly revisions, or retainer deposits) is credited directly toward your cabinet purchase. When you move forward with Seacoast Cabinet, the design is essentially free.

What if I can't visit the showroom?

No problem! Our virtual design service brings the showroom experience to you. We'll work remotely using photos and measurements to create your custom design and 3D renderings.

How long does the design process take?

Most designs are completed within 1-2 weeks after your initial design appointment. Timeline depends on project complexity and how quickly decisions are made. View our full process overview for details.

Do you only design kitchens?

While kitchens are our specialty, we also design bathrooms, laundry rooms, mudrooms, and any space that needs cabinetry. Our same design process and fee structure applies to all projects.
