What Local Permits and Regulations Affect Kitchen Remodeling in Andover MA?

Kitchen renovations in Andover often require building permits, especially when the project involves structural changes, electrical work, plumbing modifications, or changes to the home's footprint. The Town of Andover's Building Department handles permits and inspections for residential projects.

If your project involves moving walls, relocating plumbing, or updating electrical panels, your contractor will typically pull the necessary permits. Cosmetic updates like replacing cabinets and countertops on the existing layout usually don't require permits, but it's always worth confirming with the town.

Planning for permits early helps avoid scheduling delays. Our team works with Andover contractors regularly and can help you understand how the permitting process fits into your overall project timeline.
