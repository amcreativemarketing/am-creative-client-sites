Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our cabinet and design services.

What cabinet brands does Seacoast Cabinet carry?

We carry three American-made cabinet lines: Omega (fully custom), Homecrest (semi-custom), and Mantra (entry-level). All offer quality construction with options for every budget.

Do you offer free design consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary design consultations at our Rowley showroom or through our virtual design service. There's no obligation‚Äîjust an opportunity to explore your options.

What areas do you serve?

We serve homeowners and contractors throughout the North Shore of Massachusetts, Southern New Hampshire, and Southern Maine. Popular service areas include Newburyport, Haverhill, Salem NH, and Ipswich.

How long does a kitchen cabinet project take?

Timeline varies by cabinet line: Mantra ships in 1-3 weeks, Homecrest in 3-5 weeks, and Omega in 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a realistic schedule during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.
