How much do custom kitchen cabinets cost for a Salem, NH project?

Kitchen cabinet costs vary depending on kitchen size, cabinet line, and finishes chosen. Homecrest projects start at a lower investment, while fully custom Omega kitchens run higher. We provide a detailed, line-item estimate during your design consultation so you know exactly what you're spending. Our budget planner can give you a preliminary range.

Most projects take 4-8 weeks from order to delivery, depending on the cabinet line and level of customization. Homecrest orders tend to arrive on the faster end, while fully custom Omega orders may take closer to 8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline estimate during the proposal stage.
