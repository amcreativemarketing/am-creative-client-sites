About Omega Cabinetry

Semi-Custom vs. Custom: Understanding Omega's Product Lines

Omega Cabinetry occupies a space that most cabinet brands don't - offering genuine semi-custom flexibility with custom-grade construction quality. Manufactured domestically by MasterBrand Cabinets (the same parent company behind Homecrest and Mantra), Omega gives homeowners access to hundreds of door styles, finish combinations, and cabinet modifications without the lead times or pricing of a fully bespoke line. The result is a cabinet that looks and feels custom, but orders and delivers on a semi-custom timeline.

American-Made Quality and Craftsmanship

Every Omega cabinet is built domestically, which means predictable lead times, consistent quality control, and no tariff-related cost surprises. Dovetail drawer boxes, soft-close hardware, and premium finish options come standard - not as upgrades. For North Shore homeowners who value quality construction and want to support American manufacturing, Omega is one of the strongest options on the market.
