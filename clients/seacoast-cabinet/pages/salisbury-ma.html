How much do custom kitchen cabinets cost in Massachusetts?

Custom kitchen cabinet costs in Massachusetts typically range from $8,000-$15,000 for smaller or entry-level projects, $15,000-$30,000 for mid-range full kitchen renovations, and $30,000-$50,000+ for larger or fully custom projects. Your cost depends on kitchen size, cabinet line, finish selections, hardware choices, and installation complexity. We provide detailed, no-obligation estimates after your design consultation so you know exactly what you're working with.
