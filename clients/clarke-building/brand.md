## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Short Description
Clarke Building Co. is a trusted residential remodeling and construction company based in West Newbury, MA - specializing in kitchen remodels, home renovations, and ADUs across the North Shore. Reliable, skilled experts who take pride in your home as if it were their own.

### Trust Promise
"Reliable skilled experts that take pride in your home as if it were their own." *(Dave flagged this as a starting point to workshop)*

### Why Clients Choose Clarke
- Dave is the single point of contact throughout the entire project
- Calm, reassuring demeanor - especially on insurance/damage calls
- Transparent about pricing differences and how decisions affect outcomes
- Explains the integral parts of the project clearly
- Gives options with honest cost/impact guidance

### What Customers Need to Feel
- Reassured
- Understood

### Customer Fears & Frustrations
- Not understanding the process or how to get started
- Uncertainty about timelines

### Ideal Client
Laid back, understanding of the construction process, communicative enough to answer questions but not overly demanding on weekends about incomplete items.

### Top Goals
Higher organic traffic to enable higher quality leads and growth.
