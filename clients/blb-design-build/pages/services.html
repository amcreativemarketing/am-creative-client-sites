Why Design-Build Works

The traditional approach, hiring a designer and a builder separately, often leads to miscommunication, change orders, and budgets that drift. Design-build eliminates that gap by keeping design and construction under one roof from day one.

At BLB Design / Build, that means:

  • Aligned vision and budget. Our team develops your design with buildability and cost in mind from the start, so your plans don't outpace your investment.
  • One point of contact. Your project manager coordinates every trade, every timeline, and every decision, so you always know what's happening and when.
  • Fewer surprises. With our scope development process, we identify challenges early and address them before construction begins.
  • Faster completion. Design and pre-construction planning overlap, which means your project moves from concept to reality more efficiently.
