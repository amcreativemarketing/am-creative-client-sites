Local Expertise, Lasting Results

Based in Amesbury, BLB Design/Build serves homeowners across the North Shore of Massachusetts and Southern New Hampshire. Our team brings design-build expertise to communities within roughly 30 miles of our office - from historic coastal towns to inland neighborhoods.

Each project reflects the unique character of its location. Whether you're updating a historic Newburyport colonial or adding space to a contemporary home in Stratham, we coordinate every detail from initial design through final inspection.
