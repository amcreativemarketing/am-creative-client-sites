The Project

This Essex homeowner wanted a classic New England farmer's porch - the kind that gives a house presence from the street and creates a real, usable space for mornings with coffee and evenings with neighbors.

Our team designed and built the porch as a single project from foundation work through finished trim. The structure was engineered for the home's existing footprint, with proper load distribution, weather-resistant materials, and finish details that match the home's architectural character.

Like every BCM project, this build came with daily photo updates, a clear scope of work, and post-completion support through Bradford Home Services.
