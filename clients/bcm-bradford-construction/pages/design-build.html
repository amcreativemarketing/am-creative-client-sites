The Design-Build Advantage

One Team. One Contract. Zero Finger-Pointing.

Design-build means your architect and builder work as a single integrated team. No miscommunication between separate companies. No blame-shifting when issues arise. Just one point of accountability from first sketch to final walkthrough.

Traditional projects involve separate contracts with architects, general contractors, and subcontractors. When problems arise, each party points to the others. Design decisions get made without construction costs in mind. You're left managing the coordination.

With design-build, we handle everything. Design decisions are made with real-world feasibility and costs in mind from day one. When something needs attention, there's no question who's responsible.

Design-build is a project delivery method where one company handles both design and construction under a single contract. You work with one integrated team from concept through completion, eliminating the finger-pointing that happens when teams are separate.

With traditional construction, you hire an architect to create plans, then bid those plans to contractors. When problems arise, the architect blames the contractor and vice versa. With design-build, one team is accountable for everything.

Projects typically start at $300,000+ for major additions and whole-home renovations. Custom homes vary based on size, finishes, and site conditions. The best way to understand your specific project cost is through a free feasibility consultation.

Timeline varies by scope. Design typically takes 6-12 weeks, permitting 4-8 weeks depending on your town, and construction 4-9 months for major projects. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Our contracts include complete scope definition, all design work, permit fees and inspections, construction labor and materials, project management, daily documentation, site cleanup, and warranty. Allowances are provided for homeowner-selected items.
