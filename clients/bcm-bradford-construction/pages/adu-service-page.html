Making the Right Choice

Is an ADU Right for You?

ADUs are not for every situation. Here is how to know if one makes sense for your property and goals.

Accessory Dwelling Unit

Separate, self-contained living space

  • Best for creating independent living space with rental potential
  • Fully separate entrance, kitchen, bathroom, and often utilities
  • Often faster permitting under new MA and NH ADU laws
  • Adds a flexible income-producing asset to your property
  • Maximum privacy for occupants and homeowners
  • ICF build time: approximately 1 month once permitted

Home Addition

Expanding your existing home

  • Best for adding bedrooms, living space, or expanding your kitchen
  • Integrates with your existing home's systems and flow
  • Standard building permit process through your municipality
  • Increases your primary home's total square footage and value
  • Shared living for guests or family who want to be part of the household
  • Timeline varies based on scope and complexity
