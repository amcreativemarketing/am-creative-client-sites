Our Story

Building for Families,
By a Family

Rick White founded Bradford Construction Management because he kept watching families get burned. Vague scopes. Disappeared contractors. Budgets that ballooned without explanation. As a father and a construction professional with a commercial project management background, he knew homeowners deserved better.

"Your home isn't just an investment. It's where your kids grow up, where your parents age in place, where memories are made. It deserves the same discipline we'd give a $10 million commercial project."

BCM was built on that belief. Every project gets a defined scope, a clear budget, and a project manager you can actually reach. Daily photos keep you informed without requiring you to stop by the job site. And when construction wraps, Bradford Home Services steps in so the relationship - and the work - stays dialed-in for the long run.

Today, Bradford serves homeowners across the North Shore, Merrimack Valley, and Southern NH, delivering home additions, ADU construction, whole-home renovations, custom homes, and kitchen and bathroom remodels with the same commercial-grade rigor on every job.
