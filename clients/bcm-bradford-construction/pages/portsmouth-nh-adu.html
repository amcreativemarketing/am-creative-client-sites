Portsmouth, NH ADU Zoning Rules and Permits

New Hampshire's HB 577 requires municipalities to allow ADUs by right. Here's what that means for Portsmouth homeowners.

Yes. Under HB 577 (effective July 2025), Portsmouth must allow both attached and detached ADUs by right in all residential zones. This is a permitted use - you don't need variances, conditional use permits, or special exceptions, just a standard building permit.

HB 577 allows ADUs up to 800 square feet (or 1,000 sq ft if attached to the main house). Basement conversions, garage conversions, and detached cottages all qualify. Portsmouth's zoning requires minimum lot size and setbacks, but under HB 577, existing non-conforming structures can be converted.

Typical timeframe is 4-8 weeks from submission to permit issuance. HB 577 streamlines the process. We manage all submissions, questions, and revisions so you don't have to deal with the paperwork.
