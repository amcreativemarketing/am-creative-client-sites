Why Families Choose an ADU

The Solution Isn't a New House. It's a New Way to Live Together.

The Perfect Balance of Independence and Security.

For years, families have faced an impossible choice: a parent's fierce desire for independence, or a child's desperate need for peace of mind. The traditional options can force a compromise. We call our solution The Dignity of Proximity.

Dignity for Them

The freedom to live in their own private, comfortable home, on their own terms, on the property they love.

Proximity for You

The security of knowing they are just a few steps away, ending the constant worry about their safety and wellbeing.

It's not just an ADU. It's a way to keep your family close - also ideal for relatives, guests, or a rental apartment.

Our Process

The Builder That Won't Ghost You.

You have enough to worry about. Our transparent, turnkey process was designed to eliminate the stress and uncertainty from your ADU project.

1

Initial Consultation

A no-pressure call to discuss your family's needs, review the models, and answer your initial questions.

2

Site Assessment & Feasibility

We visit your property to assess the build location, discuss potential site work, and ensure a smooth project.

3

Permitting & Approvals

Our team manages the complex process of submitting construction documents and working with local zoning boards.

4

The Build

We break ground and build your turnkey ADU with daily photo updates and clear communication throughout.

5

Final Walkthrough & Move-In

A final walkthrough ensures every detail is perfect before handing over the keys.
