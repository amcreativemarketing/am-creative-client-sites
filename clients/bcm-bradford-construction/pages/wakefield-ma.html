Process

How a Project Works

Our design-build approach puts one team in charge from your first phone call through post-completion support. Click any step to learn more.

During our initial conversation, we listen to your vision, understand your timeline expectations, and discuss realistic budget parameters. This is your chance to ask questions with no pressure or obligation to move forward.

We evaluate your property's potential, review local zoning and building codes, and develop initial design concepts that fit your goals and budget. This phase determines what's actually buildable and what it will cost.

We handle all permitting details: preparing applications, managing building department reviews and revisions, and coordinating throughout the approval process so you don't have to navigate municipal bureaucracy alone.

During construction, you get daily photo updates to your project portal, all changes are documented with costs, and a dedicated project manager oversees quality and schedule. Real-time communication throughout.

After your project is complete, our partnership with Bradford Home Services means you have a dedicated maintenance team that already knows your home. Seasonal care, warranty support, and future renovations - all with one phone call.

Building in Wakefield

Wakefield sits at a practical midpoint between the North Shore coast and the Merrimack Valley, making it a natural fit for our project coverage area. Our team is familiar with Wakefield's building department, residential zoning requirements, and the mix of housing stock throughout town.

Whether you're adding a second story, renovating a kitchen that hasn't been touched in decades, or exploring an ADU for aging parents, the approach is the same: clear scope, honest pricing, reliable schedule, and a team that picks up the phone.

Our headquarters in Haverhill puts us about 30 minutes from most Wakefield addresses, with project managers on-site daily during active construction.
