How We Manage Your Residential Construction Project

From feasibility to final walkthrough, every Bradford project follows the same proven process - the one that eliminates communication gaps and keeps homeowners in control.

01

Consultation & Feasibility

We discuss your goals, assess your property and budget, and give you honest feedback on scope and timeline. No pressure - just clear answers so you can make an informed decision.

02

Design & Engineering

Our design-build approach keeps architectural planning and construction under one team. Fewer handoffs, faster decisions, one accountable point of contact.

03

Transparent Proposal

You receive a detailed, itemized proposal before any work begins. Every cost is broken down. Every change during construction requires your written approval before we proceed.

04

Permitting & Pre-Construction

We handle all permit applications, building department interactions, and inspections. You stay informed at every step, but we manage the paperwork and the process.

05

Construction & Daily Documentation

Timestamped photos delivered to you every single day. Clear milestones, coordinated trades, clean site protocols. You always know what happened on your project - no wondering.

06

Lifetime Support

After the final walkthrough, Bradford Home Services keeps us in your corner for maintenance, warranty follow-through, and ongoing support. We're the contractor who never leaves.
