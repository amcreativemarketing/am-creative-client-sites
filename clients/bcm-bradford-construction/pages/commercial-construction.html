How It Works

Our Commercial Construction Process

Every Bradford CM commercial project follows a structured, transparent process designed to protect your investment and deliver results on time and on budget.

01

Discovery & Scope

We meet to understand your goals, timeline, and budget, reviewing existing plans and identifying site challenges before any work begins.

02

Pre-Construction Planning

Detailed project plan with cost estimates, schedules, subcontractor requirements, and permit applications. Value engineering offered where applicable.

03

Permitting & Approvals

Bradford manages the full permitting process with local municipalities, ensuring your project is approved and code-compliant before breaking ground.

04

Construction & Oversight

We manage all subcontractors, materials procurement, inspections, and daily site operations with regular progress updates at every stage.

05

Walkthrough & Closeout

Thorough final inspection, complete punch list, and full documentation handoff including warranties and as-built records before we hand over the keys.
