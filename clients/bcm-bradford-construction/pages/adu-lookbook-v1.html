From Our Founder

A Foundation Built on Trust

Rick White Founder, Bradford Construction Management

If you're reading this, you're likely navigating one of the most important and sensitive decisions a family can make. You're searching for that delicate balance: a way to have peace of mind about a loved one's well-being, without taking away the independence they cherish.

I founded Bradford Construction's ADU program to solve this exact problem. I saw too many families facing the difficult choice between far-away arrangements and the constant worry of a parent living alone.

I knew there had to be a better way.

This lookbook is more than a catalog of homes; it's a guide to a new way of living together. It's about providing the peace of mind you need, and the dignity your loved ones deserve.
