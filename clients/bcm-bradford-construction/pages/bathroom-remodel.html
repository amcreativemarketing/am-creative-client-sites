Bathroom Remodel Cost in Haverhill MA - What to Expect

At Bradford Construction, we believe in transparent pricing from day one. Too many homeowners have been burned by contractors who lowball estimates and then pile on surprise costs during construction. That's not how we work.

Every project starts with a detailed, itemized proposal that breaks down materials, labor, permits, and project management. You'll know exactly what you're investing before any work begins. If you want to upgrade fixtures or add features during construction, our documented change order process ensures you approve every cost adjustment in writing.

What Affects Your Bathroom Remodel Cost:

  • Size and layout of existing bathroom
  • Structural changes (moving plumbing lines, electrical work)
  • Material selections (tile quality, fixture brands, custom vs. stock vanity)
  • Special features (heated floors, steam shower, custom tile work)
  • Massachusetts permit fees (typically $150–$400 for bathroom projects)
  • Age of home and condition of existing systems
