Andover ADU Zoning & Building Requirements

Massachusetts' Affordable Homes Act (effective February 2025) allows ADUs by right in single-family zones. Andover adopted local regulations through Article 22 at the April 2025 Town Meeting.

Maximum Size

900 sq ft or 50% of the principal dwelling's gross floor area, whichever is smaller

Approval Process

Site Plan Review by Andover's Zoning Board of Appeals (ZBA) — not a special permit, but a regulatory review

Parking

One off-street parking space required if property is more than 0.5 miles from an MBTA station

Owner Occupancy

Not required — state law prohibits owner-occupancy mandates for ADUs

Setbacks & Height

Must comply with existing zoning district setback and height requirements for your lot

One ADU Per Lot

Principal structure must be completed before ADU construction begins

Important: Zoning requirements vary by district and individual lot characteristics. Septic capacity, lot coverage limits, and conservation considerations can all affect your ADU project.
