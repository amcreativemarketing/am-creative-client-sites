### Usage Notes - Lifestyle Renders

**Mom's Place Next Door**
- Primary aging-in-place hero image
- Shows emotional connection (mother + daughter)
- Good for: Website hero, Facebook ads, lookbook cover

**Mountain Retreat**
- NH/vacation property expansion market
- Wooded/mountain setting, ski gear visible
- Good for: NH geo-targeting, vacation property messaging

**The Guest Suite (VERTICAL)**
- Rental income angle, Airbnb-ready vibe
- Two-story Maple model with roof deck
- Good for: Instagram stories, vertical ad placements, Pinterest

**Work From Home**
- Remote work/home office angle
- Modern flat roof aesthetic
- Good for: Professional audience targeting

**Winter Ready**
- Seasonal variety, year-round livability
- Cozy interior glow, light snow
- Good for: Winter campaigns, seasonal rotation

**Connected Living**
- Shows ADU + main house relationship
- Demonstrates attached/breezeway customization
- Good for: Customization messaging, "designed for your property"

**Open Living Space (Interior)**
- Answers "will it feel cramped?" objection
- Vaulted ceiling, natural light, modern finishes
- Good for: Website interior section, lookbook

**Accessible Bathroom (Interior)**
- Aging-in-place features without clinical feel
- Curbless shower, grab bars integrated into design
- Good for: Aging-in-place content, accessibility messaging

## Usage by Channel

### Website ADU Page
- Hero: Mom's Place Next Door OR Mountain Retreat (rotate by market)
- Use cases section: All lifestyle renders
- Interior section: Open Living Space, Accessible Bathroom
- Models section: All architectural renders + floor plans
- Customization: Connected Living

### Paid Ads (Aging-in-Place Funnel)
- Primary: Mom's Place Next Door
- Secondary: Accessible Bathroom, Open Living Space
- Do NOT use: Mountain Retreat, Work From Home (different ICP)

### Paid Ads (Broad/General)
- Rotate through: Mountain Retreat, Guest Suite, Work From Home
- Seasonal: Winter Ready (Nov-Feb)

### 2026 Lookbook
- Cover: Mom's Place Next Door OR DBL Rendering 3
- Model pages: Architectural renders + floor plans
- Lifestyle spread: All lifestyle renders organized by use case
- Interior spread: Both interior renders

### Social Media
- Instagram feed: All landscape renders
- Instagram stories: Guest Suite, Maple Model (vertical)
- Facebook: Lifestyle renders with people perform better

## File Naming Convention

**Lifestyle renders:** `[Model]-[Use-Case].webp`  
Example: `ELM-OHG-Moms-Place-Next-Door.webp`

**Architectural renders:** `[Model]-Rendering-[#].webp` or `[Model]-photo-render.webp`  
Example: `DBL-Rendering-3.webp`

**Floor plans:** `[Model]-floorplan.webp` or `[Model]-[floor]-floorplan.webp`  
Example: `Maple-1st-floor-floorplan.webp`
