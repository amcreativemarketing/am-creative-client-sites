Warning Signs

Does Your Panel Need an Upgrade?

Electrical problems rarely fix themselves. If you're noticing these issues, it's time for a professional assessment. Not sure? Our troubleshooting service can diagnose the root cause.

Breakers Trip Frequently

Circuit breakers that trip regularly signal your panel can't handle your home's electrical load.

Flickering or Dimming Lights

Lights that flicker or dim when appliances turn on indicate insufficient capacity.

Panel Feels Warm or Buzzes

Heat or buzzing sounds from your panel are serious warning signs of loose connections.

Relying on Power Strips

If you need power strips everywhere, you've outgrown your electrical system.

Home Built Before 1980

Older homes often need rewiring and panel upgrades for modern demands.

Planning for EV or HVAC

Adding an EV charger or heat pump? These often require 200-amp service.
