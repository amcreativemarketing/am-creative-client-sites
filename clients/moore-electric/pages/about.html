Experience & Expertise

25+ Years in the Electrical Trade

What Sets Us Apart

Moore Electric isn't the biggest electrical contractor in the area, and that's by design. Here's what you get when you hire us.

A Master Electrician License Isn't a Formality

The Master Electrician credential is the top-tier license in the trade. It requires years of verified field experience, passing rigorous state examinations, and staying current with code updates. When Brenden pulls your permit and signs off on your work, that credential is on the line.

Licensed Across NH, MA & ME

Moore Electric holds active electrical contractor licenses in New Hampshire, Massachusetts, and Maine. Whether your property is in Plaistow, Haverhill, or across the border, the work is fully licensed and code-compliant in every jurisdiction we operate in.

Permits & Utility Coordination - Handled

Pulling permits and coordinating with utilities like Eversource and Unitil is part of the job, not an upsell. Moore Electric manages all permit applications and utility communication so the work is legal, inspected, and properly documented.

Brenden's Team Does the Work. Every Time.

Moore Electric does not subcontract. The crew that shows up is Brenden's crew - trained, supervised, and accountable. You won't get a third-party team doing work in your home under someone else's license.

Free Estimates. Straight Pricing.

There's no charge to get a quote from Moore Electric, and the price you're given reflects the actual scope of the work. If something changes, you'll hear about it before it happens, not after.

BBB Accredited. Best of Newburyport.

Moore Electric is a BBB Accredited Business and a Best of Newburyport award recipient in 2024 and 2025 - reflecting a track record of reliable work and satisfied customers across the region.
