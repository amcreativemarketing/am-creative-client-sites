Commercial Electrical FAQs

Can you work around our business hours?

Yes. We understand downtime costs money. For many commercial projects, we can schedule work during off-hours, weekends, or in phases that minimize disruption to your operations.

The standards are the same - code-compliant, clean, owner-supervised. Commercial projects often involve larger panels, three-phase power, and coordination with other trades, but our attention to detail doesn't change.

Do you handle permits for commercial projects?

Absolutely. We pull all necessary permits and coordinate inspections. Being licensed in MA, NH, and ME means we know the requirements in each jurisdiction.
