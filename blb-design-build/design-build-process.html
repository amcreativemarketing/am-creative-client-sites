How Design-Build Compares

Understanding your options helps you choose the right approach for your project

Our Approach

Design-Build

One team handles everything

Design and construction work together from the start. Budget guidance begins early, and your single point of contact manages all coordination.

Best for homeowners who want accountability, efficiency, and a streamlined experience.

Traditional

Architect + Contractor

Separate design and build phases

You hire an architect for drawings, then bid the project to contractors. More control over each phase, but you manage coordination between parties.

Can work well when design complexity requires specialized architectural expertise.

Common Questions

What's included in the initial consultation?

Your consultation includes a site walk, discussion of your goals and priorities, photographic documentation, and preliminary budget conversation. You'll receive a written scope statement and budget range to guide next steps.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline varies based on project scope and complexity. During your consultation, we'll discuss realistic expectations for your specific project. Schedule a consultation to learn more.

How do you handle changes during construction?

Change orders are documented, priced, and approved before any work proceeds. Our transparent change-order log shows exactly how modifications affect your timeline and budget.

What areas do you serve?

BLB Design/Build serves homeowners within 30 miles of our Amesbury office, including Newburyport, West Newbury, Newbury, Rowley, Boxford, and Georgetown. Contact us to confirm we serve your area.

Do you work on historic homes?

Yes. Many North Shore homes have historic character that requires careful renovation approaches. Our team coordinates with specialized trades experienced in historic preservation.
